Apple's main products are the Mac line, including laptops and desktops, the iPad line of multipurpose tablets, and wearables, home and accessories, featuring smartwatches and wireless headphones.

Tim Cook notes that Apple's installed base of customers continues to grow, driving engagement in the ecosystem and benefiting various services categories. This growth is fueled by a combination of factors, including the addition of new content, features, and storage plans. As the company expands its range of products and services, it is also refining its approach to consumer education and launch strategies, as evident in its plans for the upcoming Vision Pro product.
